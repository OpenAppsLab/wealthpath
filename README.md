# WealthPath IRR Dashboard

WealthPath is a self-contained personal finance and investment planning app for tracking wealth, estimating investment returns, and planning for long-term financial goals.

## What this app does

- Track investments across property, shares, ETFs, crypto, gold, bonds, term deposits, and cash
- Enter purchase price, current value, fees, distributions, and holding period to estimate IRR
- Review income, expenses, superannuation, and retirement pathway in one workspace
- Compare current holdings with recommended alternatives and performance insights
- Import and export your data as JSON for easy backup and reuse

## How to use it

1. Open WealthPath-fixed.html in your browser.
2. Choose whether you are a new or existing user.
3. Add your investment details and build your portfolio view.
4. Review the summary, insights, and retirement pathway sections.

## Notes

This app is designed as a lightweight, browser-based dashboard and does not require a backend or external database.
